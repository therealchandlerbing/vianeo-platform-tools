## Step 3 Overview

### The 29 Questions Map To:

**LEGITIMACY** (15% weight, threshold 3.0)
- Q8: Problem definition and evidence
- Q13: Field of application clarity

**DESIRABILITY** (25% weight, threshold 3.5)
- Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4: User preferences and needs
- Q9, Q10: Engagement and retention
- Q14, Q15, Q16: Customer studies and behavior
- Q21, Q22: Market trends and substitutes
- Q27: Market expansion potential

**ACCEPTABILITY** (20% weight, threshold 3.0)
- Q5, Q6, Q7: Partners, dependencies, influencers
- Q17, Q18, Q19: Geographic, technology, network factors

**FEASIBILITY** (20% weight, threshold 3.0)
- Q11, Q12: Technical development and skills
- Q24, Q25, Q28: Speed, innovation, capital efficiency

**VIABILITY** (20% weight, threshold 3.0)
- Q20: Market size sufficiency
- Q23: Value capture efficiency
- Q26: Profitability timeline
- Q29: Follow-on funding access

### Scoring Scale

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 5 | Absolutely - strong external validation |
| 4 | Almost - good evidence |
| 3 | Likely - basic validation |
| 2 | Maybe not - weak evidence |
| 1 | Don't know - no evidence |
