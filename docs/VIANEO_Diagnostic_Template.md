### Body Paragraphs
```

  - 11pt, body gray (#2D2D2D)
  - Line height: 1.6x (360 DXA spacing)

### Dimension Summary Table
```
Table structure:
  - 3 columns: Dimension (3120 DXA) | Score (1560 DXA) | Interpretation (4680 DXA)

  - Green (#D4EDDA): Scores ≥3.5

```

  - Bullet character: "•"
  - Indent: 720pt left, 360pt hanging
  - Spacing: inherit from paragraph style

### Footer
```

  - Evidence Sources: [Bold label] + content

## Content Specifications

### Executive Diagnostic Section

**Total length:** 6-8 sentences across 4 paragraphs

**Strengths:** 1-2 sentences
- Must include: specific numbers, names, concrete evidence
- Lead with strongest validation
- No generic statements

**Risks:** 1-2 sentences
- Must include: quantification where possible
- Direct language, no hedging
- Prioritized by impact

**Near-term Actions:** 2-3 sentences
- Must include: 3-4 actions with owners in parentheses
- Delegation-ready language
- Measurable outcomes

**Evidence Gaps:** 1-2 sentences
- Must include: material gaps only
- Grouped by category
- Specific about what's missing

### Dimension Summary

**Required elements:**
- All 5 dimensions with scores
- Status keyword + brief explanation for each
- Overall status statement below table

**Status keywords:**
- Strong (4.5-5.0)
- Promising (3.5-4.4)
- Developing (3.0-3.4)
- Problematic (2.0-2.9)
- Non-viable (<2.0)

### Critical Path Forward

**Required sections (4 total):**
1. Immediate Priority (Weeks 1-4): Max 3 actions
2. Short-term Priority (Months 2-3): Max 4 actions
3. Medium-term Priority (Months 4-6): Max 4 actions
4. Success Metrics: Max 6 metrics

**Action quality:**
- Specific and measurable
- Time-bound
- Addresses identified gap or capitalizes on strength

**Metric quality:**
- Specific thresholds
- Measurable
- Timeframes included where applicable

### Footer Metadata

**Required elements:**
1. Assessment Methodology (list all frameworks)
2. Evidence Sources (list all documents)
3. Next Review (date + trigger)
