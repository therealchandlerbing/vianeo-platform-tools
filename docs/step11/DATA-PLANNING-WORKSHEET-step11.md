## Tips for Completing This Worksheet

1. **Gather Team Input**: Don't complete this alone. Get input from technical leads, product managers, and stakeholders.

2. **Be Specific**: Instead of "External API", specify "Stripe Payment API" or "Google Maps API".

3. **Think Realistically**: Consider actual availability of resources, not just wishful thinking.

4. **Prioritize Ruthlessly**: Not all features are equally important. Use opportunity levels strategically.

5. **Consider Timing**: Be honest about implementation timelines. Overly optimistic timelines lead to missed expectations.

6. **Document Assumptions**: If you're unsure about a partner or means requirement, note it in Implementation Notes.

7. **Review with Experts**: Have technical leads review means requirements, and business development review partner relationships.

8. **Update Regularly**: This is a living document. As you learn more, update the worksheet and regenerate the matrix.
